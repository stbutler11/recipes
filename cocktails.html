Espresso Martini

Ingredients

  • 40ml Vodka (Vanilla if possible)
  • 20ml Kahlua
  • 20ml Espresso
  • 2 heaped teaspoons of sugar
  • Frothed milk or cream
  • 3 coffee beans for garnish

Method

  • Fill a shaker with ice cubes
  • Add all the ingredients
  • Shake
  • Strain into cocktail glass
  • Garnish with coffee beans

Espresso Martini

Ingredients

  • 75ml whisky
  • 75ml lemon
  • 75ml egg white
  • 3 spoons of sugar

Method

  • Add all the ingredients to a shaker
  • Shake
  • Top up shaker with ice
  • Shake
  • Strain over ice
